Mutually inclusive actions: actions in which one cannot happen without the other.

Actions A & B are mutually inclusive if A requires one or more effects from B and B requires one or more effects from A.

===

pc1.refer = a
2 0
9 2
7 -1 2

pc2.refer = a
2 0
7 2
9 -1 2

pc.refer = a
2 0
7 -1 2
9 -1 2

===

pc1.refer = b
5 0
9 1
7 -1 1

pc2.refer = b
5 0
7 1
9 -1 1
